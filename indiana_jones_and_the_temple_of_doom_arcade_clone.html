HOW TO PLAY

The Classic Atari System 1 Rules Refined

STAGE 1 MINES: RESCUE CHILDREN

Explore the horizontal subterranean caverns. Free children trapped in cages by whipping them. Climb ladders, use conveyor belts, dodge moving spiked logs, avoid slithering cobras, and whip peg posts to swing safely across glowing volcanic lava traps.

STAGE 2 MINECART SPEED RUN

Hurtle down the hazardous rail tracks in a runaway minecart. Tilt/shift tracks (Up/Down) to avoid massive boulders and bottomless track gaps. Smash enemy Thuggee carts out of the way before they crash into you!

STAGE 3 KALI TEMPLE: SANKARA STONE

Ascend the grand platform altar. Retrieve the mystical, glowing Sankara Stone standing on the ritual fire pedestal. Beware of breaking tiles and Mola Ram materializing out of thin air to throw burning projectile skulls!

STAGE 4 ROPE BRIDGE FINALE

On the 4th cycle repetition, the temple is replaced by the climactic cliffside rope bridge. Defend yourself against Thuggees attacking from both sides and Mola Ram's heavy fire attacks. Crack your whip at the bridge cables to snap the bridge, sending Mola Ram into the gorge!
